# Market Bands — reference for Step 4

The market band is the reality check on your value-derived candidate fee. This
file explains how to build one, plus illustrative 2026 benchmarks for common
service types. **Treat the numbers as directional starting points**, not gospel —
the user's own won deals and live competitor pricing are always the best comp.

---

## How to build a band (do this first)

1. **Gather 5–10 real comparables.** Sources: competitor websites and rate cards,
   published course/workshop prices, RFP/procurement ranges, peer and association
   benchmarks, freelancer-marketplace floors for commoditized versions of the work.
2. **Sort into low / typical / high.** Ignore the extreme outliers at first.
3. **Translate agency → solo.** Most published pricing is for agencies/firms. A
   solo operator anchors to the *freelance/independent* band, not the agency band.
4. **Add the specialist premium.** Domain specialists command ~20–40% over
   generalists — enough to sit in the upper half of the freelance band.
5. **No comps? Generate the band from the audience** via Van Westendorp
   willingness-to-pay questions.

## Illustrative 2026 benchmarks (US)

### Independent consulting / advisory
- Day rate: **$600–$1,200/day**; senior specialists **$1,500+**
- Short strategy/advisory project: **$10K–$30K**
- Monthly retainer (solo): **$3K–$10K**; light advisory $2–5K, intensive $8–15K

### Workshops & corporate training
- Solo expert workshop: **$3K–$6K per day** in fees
- In-person, per participant: **$1K–$3K/day/person**
- Department-level program: **$3K–$25K**
- Executive-alignment program: **$15K–$50K**
- Org-wide / multi-department program: **$12K–$250K**
- *The ladder is the lever:* the same expertise packaged one rung up
  (workshop → program → executive engagement) is 3–8× the deal.

### Cohort courses (professional audience)
- Entry: **$400–$800**
- Typical: **$800–$2,500** (platform-stated range)
- Premium / certificate tier: **$2,500–$4,000+**
- Professional/executive audiences support the upper half of "typical."

### PR / communications retainers (context)
- Boutique / solo publicist: **$2K–$7.5K/month**
- Mid-size agency: **$10K–$25K/month**
- Enterprise: **$25K–$50K+/month**
- Project (e.g. launch): **$15K–$75K**
- Useful even if you don't sell PR: it's often the price anchor already in a
  comms buyer's head.

## Reconciling with the value number

- Candidate fee **inside** the band → ship it.
- Candidate fee **above** the band → cap at/near the top; bank the excess value as
  your proof/case-study, not your invoice.
- Candidate fee **below** the band → raise toward the band; you're underpricing.

The band constrains; the value justifies; your proof decides where in the band
you land.
